I spent a focused weekend **'Vibe Coding'** this project—obsessing over every shadow, haptic pulse, and tapered edge until it felt less like an app and more like a high-end physical companion. No tracking, no cloud, no bloat. Just you, your dhikr, and a world-class interface."

## 💎 Premium Features
- **Tactile Vibe**: Realistic 3D molded body with an ergonomic "Tapered Pebble" shape.
- **Privacy First**: **No-Cloud, No-Analytics, No-BS.** Your counting stays on your device.
- **Glassmorphism**: Authentic LCD screen with bezel depth and glass glare.
- **Haptic Harmony**: Specifically tuned vibration patterns for every tap and reset.
- **Auto-Memory**: Your counts and goals persist across restarts.
- **Friday Spacial**: Automated greetings and reminders for Surah al-Kahf.
